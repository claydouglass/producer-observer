## Questions (in order)

### 1. How am I doing at Miracle Greens?
**Answer:** "#1 of 50 brands. $22,202 revenue."
**Chart:** KPI cards (rank, revenue, transactions, customers)

### 2. Am I going up or down?
**Answer:** "Down 65% from your September peak."
**Chart:** Monthly revenue trend with forecast

### 3. Who's beating me?
**Answer:** "NW Kind, Freshy are behind you. You're #1."
**Chart:** Competitor ranking list (FREE - addictive)

### 4. How's my rank changing over time?
**Answer:** "You peaked at #3 in September, now #1."
**Chart:** RankHistory component

### 5. What's selling?
**Answer:** "Flower is 82% of your revenue at $18,324."
**Chart:** Category + Type breakdown cards

### 6. Which products are winning?
**Answer:** "Honeycomb Pave is your top seller at $1,554."
**Chart:** Product list - winners highlighted, losers flagged

### 7. Which products should I cut?
**Answer:** "5 products made <$50 total. Consider discontinuing."
**Chart:** Underperforming products with "Cut?" flag

### 8. Is my pricing right?
**Answer:** "You're 17% Premium, 0.3% Ultra. Gap exists."
**Chart:** Tier breakdown vs market

### 9. When do I sell most?
**Answer:** "Week 38 was peak. 87% swing week to week."
**Chart:** Weekly velocity sparkline

### 10. What's the opportunity here?
**Answer:** "Concentrates growing. Flower needs attention."
**Chart:** Category trends with recommendations
